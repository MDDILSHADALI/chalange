Green Energy

Green energy comes from natural sources such as sunlight, wind, rain, tides, plants,
algae and geothermal heat. Theres energy resources are renewable energy surces
also have a much smaller impact on the environment then fossil fuels,
which produce pollutantss such as greenhouse gases as a by-product, contributing ti climate change.
Green energy, however, utilizes energy sources that are readily available all over the world,
including in rural and remote areas that don't otherwise have access to electrcity.
Green energy can replace fossil fuels in all major areas of use includin electricity,
water and space heating and fuel for motor wehicles.
